Welcome to Peace & Wisdom

A sanctuary of Buddhist teachings in New Delhi

OUR VISION

To preserve and share the ancient wisdom of Cambodian Buddhism, fostering peace, compassion, and mindfulness in the heart of New Delhi.

Meditation

Join our daily meditation sessions rooted in Theravada tradition. Experience inner peace through guided meditation and mindfulness practices.

Dharma Teachings

Learn from our resident monks about Buddhist philosophy, ethics, and the path to enlightenment through weekly Dharma discussions.

Venerable Monks

Our monastery is blessed with learned monks from Cambodia who guide the community with wisdom, compassion, and traditional teachings.

Activity

Our Monastery hosts regular Buddhist ceremonies, offerings, chanting, and Dhamma talks led by Cambodian monks, community with wisdom and compassion.

FOUNDER OF TEMPLE

The Indo-Khmer Theravada Buddhist Society of New Delhi was established in 1991 to promote the teachings of Theravada Buddhism, foster cultural ties between India and Cambodia. We are committed to preserving Buddhist heritage, supporting community welfare, and spreading the values of peace, compassion, and wisdom.
